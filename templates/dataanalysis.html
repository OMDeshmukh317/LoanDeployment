Data Analysis

This Page provides an overview of the data analysis conducted on the Loan dataset. The analysis aims to explore and understand the underlying patterns, relationships, and distributions within the data. Various visualizations such as histograms, bar charts, pie charts, and violinplots are used to present insights in an intuitive and meaningful way. The univariate analysis focuses on individual features to identify trends and anomalies, while the bivariate analysis examines relationships between pairs of variables, such as the impact of demographic factors on loan approval status.
